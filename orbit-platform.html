The Framework

Sense → Solve → Engage → Repeat

Every Orbit agent runs a continuous agentic loop — not a rules engine. It reads signals, reasons about the best action, and engages donors in ways that feel genuinely personal.

01 / SENSE 📡
Read the Environment

Agents ingest giving history, wealth signals, engagement behavior, communication preferences, life events, and seasonal factors — continuously updating a living donor model.

02 / SOLVE 🧠
Reason & Decide

Agents weigh cultivation stage, donor sentiment, channel, tone, timing, and ask amount — dynamically choosing the highest-impact next move for each individual donor.

03 / ENGAGE ✉️
Personalized Outreach

Agents reach donors via email, SMS, handwritten note robotics, and phone — always in your organization's voice, with full AI transparency and donor opt-in consent.

04 / LISTEN 👂
Respond in Real Time

Agents process donor replies, update sentiment models, escalate to human fundraisers when right, and continuously refine their approach based on what actually works.

05 / CLOSE 🤝
Gift Discovery & Documentation

Agents guide donors through Smart Gift Agreements — multi-year pledges, one-time gifts, planned giving — and route to human gift officers at the right moment.

06 / STEWARD 💌
Perpetual Stewardship

The relationship never ends. Agents deliver impact updates, milestone recognition, and meaningful touchpoints year-round — converting one-time donors into lifelong advocates.

VEO · Virtual Engagement Officer

Turn prospects into donors, at scale

The VEO manages portfolios of 500–2,000 prospects that human gift officers simply can't reach. It builds genuine relationships through personalized moves-management journeys — with giving as the natural outcome.

  • Personalized 8–14 touchpoint donor journeys using moves-management strategy
  • Intelligent ask amount calibration based on capacity, history, and peer benchmarks
  • Lapsed donor re-engagement with individualized reconnection narratives
  • Mid-level donor upgrade pathways toward major gift qualification
  • Matching gift discovery and facilitation integrated into outreach flows
  • Real-time human handoff for complex situations or major gift thresholds
  • Full transparency — always discloses AI identity with complete donor opt-in

VSO · Virtual Stewardship Officer

World-class stewardship for every donor

Stewardship is the strongest predictor of donor retention — yet most organizations only deliver it meaningfully to their top 3%. The VSO changes that, delivering personalized impact acknowledgment to every donor in your database, perpetually.

  • Automated personalized impact updates tied to specific funded programs
  • Anniversary, milestone, and tribute acknowledgments at scale
  • Pledge reminder and fulfillment flows with warm, personal nudges
  • Lapsed stewardship reactivation before donors fully disengage
  • Perpetual stewardship for donors who have made their final major gift
  • Multi-channel delivery: email, SMS, and handwritten note robotics
  • Donor sentiment monitoring with escalation triggers to human staff

VPGO · Virtual Planned Giving Officer

Surface bequest conversations at scale

Planned giving is the most underleveraged revenue category in nonprofit fundraising — primarily because human capacity to identify and cultivate legacy prospects is so limited. The VPGO changes the math entirely.

  • Predictive planned giving prospect identification from your existing database
  • Empathetic, age-appropriate legacy conversation initiation
  • Educational content delivery on bequest types, tax benefits, and estate planning
  • Legacy society recruitment and recognition flows
  • Attorney referral facilitation and document support coordination
  • Long-horizon relationship maintenance over multi-year bequest cultivation
  • Human PGFO warm handoff at appropriate conversation milestones

VCO · Virtual Campaign Officer

Run every campaign with a full team's power

The VCO orchestrates time-sensitive campaigns — Giving Tuesday, day-of-giving, year-end, and capital campaigns — with personalized outreach that mass email tools can't match, at a scale your staff never could.

  • Multi-channel campaign orchestration: email, SMS, and direct mail triggers
  • Real-time performance monitoring with autonomous tactical adjustments
  • Matching gift challenge facilitation and lead gift announcement sequences
  • Peer-to-peer fundraising volunteer recruitment and live coaching
  • Countdown urgency sequences personalized by donor segment
  • Post-campaign impact reporting and next-cycle donor seeding
